Banku

Description

Banku with hot pepper and fried fish

Ingredients

  • corn dough
  • Oil
  • Cassava dough
  • Fresh Fish
  • Pepper, Tomatoes & onions

Steps

  1. Mix cassava and corm dough
  2. Put on fire and stir until it becomes difficult to stir
  3. Turn it over continuously until it sticks together
  4. Fry fish with oil
  5. Grind pepper, tomatoes and onions together
  6. Mould Banku in round shape and serve with pepper and fish
